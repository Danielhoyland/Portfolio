What I Have Learned

This subject focused heavily on low-level programming, which was a significant shift from my other courses. Initially, it was challenging, but as time went on, it became much more enjoyable and manageable. I gained extensive knowledge in object creation through programming, camera movements, rendering, shading, texturing, and applying lighting effects in graphical programming. Additionally, I became more conscious and aware of memory management and how to prevent memory leaks throughout this course.
