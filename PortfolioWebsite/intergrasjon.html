Integration Project (aka. Mini Bachelor)

PROG2052

Subject Description

In this course the students have a group project where they combine and expand themes from a number of individual topics in their study program. In their project, the students will integrate and work with themes such as mobile/web-applications, distributed/centralized solutions, graphics/game-programming, artificial intelligence, database, cloud technologies, networking or virtualization.

Through the project work, the students will work with agile development processes, project planning, requirements specification, design, implementation, testing, sustainability, artificial intelligence, information security and documentation.

Read more at the subject site

What I Have Learned

This project was the first undertaking of our studies, where we integrated different technologies to create a cohesive product. Additionally, we collaborated as a team, which provided a more authentic experience compared to a traditional classroom setting. From this project, I learned about agile development practices, specifically Scrum, and gained proficiency in React. I also discovered how to connect a website to a database and effectively manage data retrieval without pulling too much or too little information.
